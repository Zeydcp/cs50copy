Interests

Beyond my professional and volunteer endeavors, I find joy in a range of interests that enrich my life. Exploring the world through photography has become a beloved pastime, allowing me to capture moments of beauty and significance. Additionally, I'm an avid reader, drawn to literature that delves into complex moral dilemmas and the intricacies of the human experience. Physical activity is another integral part of my routine; whether it's early morning runs or practicing yoga, staying healthy and centered is essential to navigate the challenges that come my way. These interests not only provide me with avenues for personal growth but also remind me of the diverse and vibrant world we inhabit, inspiring both my journalistic work and my commitment to positive change.
